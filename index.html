La Besnardiere (The Bear Keeper's Cottage) is a 17th century farmhouse and gite centered around a large courtyard, typical of the Maine et Loire region, set in five acres of French countryside.

The farmhouse has original oak-beamed rooms and inglenook fireplaces and is available for a welcoming B&B.
Across the courtyard is the gite accommodation which has been beautifully restored to create light and spacious accommodation for an enjoyable and relaxing holiday.
One of the joys of a visit is the safe spacious area in which children can relax and play in safety with child friendly animals just waiting for their attention.

The Gite

Gite HolidayThe gite is a converted barn which has been beautifully restored to provide relaxing accommodation.

The Farmhouse Bed & Breakfast Farmhouse Bed & BreakfastThe farmhouse has comfortable furnished double and twin bedrooms, bathroom, lounge and dining room.

Meals

Beautiful Surroundings Spacious Outside Patio for the house Playing under the willow tree Eating in the shade Bed and breakfast comes with a hearty breakfast, a three course evening meal may be booked in the morning for B&B residents and self-catering guests. The dining room offers whole food and vegetarian meals prepared from fresh organically grown vegetables from our own kitchen garden with eggs from our free range chickens. In the summer meals are served "al fresco" under the trees or in the open barn, with the light of the stars and candles on the tables. Many a good evening has been spent eating a delicious three course meal, emptying a bottle of wine and enjoying the company of new and old friends all whilst being serenaded by a choir of cicadas and crickets.

Special Events & Workshops

The whole accommodation has a large studio space that may be hired for workshops for catered group stays and events.

During a group stay all the premises are used in the farmhouse and gite. Twelve people can easily be accommodated and many more if camping is included. The converted barn has an ideal workshop space and groups that have taken advantage of this space include Art, Crafts, Yoga, Dance, Meditation and Massage. It has also been used for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries and other celebratory parties.

All meals are provided and are served to suit the times of the groups needs, but if self catering is prefered a fully equiped kitchen is available for use. Group leaders are invited to enquire about special terms for groups and parties.

Therapies

Healing massage aromatherapy and reflexology are available from resident qualified practitioner.
